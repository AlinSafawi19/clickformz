How We Use Cookies

At ClickFormz, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide essential functionality. Here's how we use cookies on our website:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

  • Cookie consent preferences
  • Language preferences
  • Session management

Preference Cookies

These cookies enable the website to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks, like your preferred language or the region you are in.

  • Language settings (English/Arabic)
  • Recent searches
  • Form preferences and settings

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

  • Page visit statistics
  • User behavior analysis
  • Website performance metrics

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our website and some services and functionalities may not work.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please visit this Cookie Policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.
